Life is tough and sometimes we just need some therapy to help us through it and that is okay! My approach is warm, honest, and client-centered.


When you come to see me, I will be non-judgmental, you will set your goals, and we will work at your own pace. I will help navigate you through obstacles and life's challenges to reach your goals. I am also bilingual, fluent in English and Spanish, I offer services in both languages. I can also help with any issues you may face within your culture.


I have experience working in family preservations and with families who have endured a lot of trauma. This has allowed me to work with clients of all ages. Send me an email or leave a voicemail if you are interested in services for yourself or a loved one, or if you have any questions.
